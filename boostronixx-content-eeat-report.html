7. AI Search Optimization & GEO Readiness

Context (May 2026): Google AI Mode launched publicly in May 2025 across 180+ countries. Unlike AI Overviews, AI Mode provides fully conversational search with zero traditional blue links — AI citation is the only visibility mechanism. BoostronixX must optimize for both AI Overviews and AI Mode to remain visible in the evolving search landscape.

Quotable Statements

45/100

Blog posts have quotable stats (2.5× growth, 77% brand recognition) but none are attributed to verifiable sources. AI systems prefer citing content with traceable claims. Service pages have no citable statistics at all.

Structured Answer Formats

50/100

Blog posts use Q&A FAQ sections and numbered steps — good. Service pages have FAQs only on SEO, Website Design, and Social Media pages. Missing on Brand Consulting, Digital Marketing, Performance Marketing.

Entity Clarity

25/100

Organization schema missing. No "BoostronixX" entity defined anywhere in structured data. AI systems cannot reliably attribute content to the brand without clear entity definitions.

Topical Authority Signals

40/100

Brand strategy cluster is growing (6 posts). But no content clusters for SEO, Performance Marketing, Social Media, or Website Design services. Single-page service coverage without informational depth.

First-Party Data

10/100

No original research, proprietary data, or unique statistics produced by BoostronixX. AI systems heavily favor citing original data sources. This is the highest-leverage gap to address.

Schema / Structured Data

15/100

No JSON-LD schema detected. No Organization, LocalBusiness, FAQ, Article, or Service schema. Schema markup helps AI systems parse and attribute content — zero schema is a critical AI visibility gap.

AI Optimization Action Plan

  1. Add Organization + LocalBusiness schema with sameAs social profiles

    AI systems use sameAs links (LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram) to build entity graphs. Without this, "BoostronixX" is an anonymous entity that AI systems can't reliably cite or recommend.

    Critical~1 hour

  2. Add answer-first formatting to all service pages

    AI Overviews pull from the first 2–3 sentences of a page. Rewrite service page introductions to directly answer "What does [service] include?" in the first sentence. Example: "BoostronixX's SEO services include technical audits, keyword strategy, link building, and local SEO — specifically designed for Jaipur businesses in real estate, education, and D2C."

    High2–3 hours

  3. Cite sources for all statistics in blog posts

    Blog post cites Lucidpress (23% revenue increase), Forbes (77% purchase decisions), University of Loyola (80% brand recognition) — none are linked. AI systems and Google raters check source credibility. Add hyperlinks to primary sources for every statistic.

    High1–2 hours per post

  4. Publish original data: "State of Branding for Indian SMBs 2026"

    Survey 50–100 Indian SMB founders on their branding challenges and budgets. Publish findings as a blog post + downloadable PDF. Original research is the single most powerful AI citation signal and natural backlink magnet.

    HighSubstantial (1–2 weeks)

  5. Create an llms.txt file

    Add /llms.txt to the root of the site with a plain-text summary of what BoostronixX is, what services it offers, and where the key content lives. Perplexity, ChatGPT, and other AI crawlers use this file to understand site context. This is a 15-minute task with outsized AI visibility impact.

    Medium15 min

  6. Add Article schema + author Person schema to all blog posts

    Google Discover, Google AI Overviews, and AI Mode all use Article schema to identify citable content. Author Person schema builds E-E-A-T entity signals. Required for AI Discover cards and improved Google AI Mode attribution.

    HighHalf day
